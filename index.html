Short Bio

Roberto Louis Forestal (費瑞斯) is a Lecturer at Chung Yuan Christian University (CYCU), Taiwan. He obtained his Doctor of Philosophy in Business with a major in Information Management. After a brief period as a Research Assistant at CYCU, in 2021 he joined the International Undergraduate Program in Business and Management (IUBM) at the College of Business, where he currently teaches courses such as Mathematics for Business Applications and Organizational Behavior. His research interests are in the area of Technology, operations management, corporate finance, and emerging markets. He also worked on topics of music information processing, nanotechnology, blockchain technology, and prediction markets.
