Roberto L. Forestal

Teaching experience

Roberto L. Forestal is a faculty member of the College of Business, Chung Yuan Christian University in Taiwan. He has experience in teaching courses such as Mathematics for Business Applications and Organizational Behavior. His teaching interests are international finance, investments, patent analysis, organizational behavior and emerging markets.

Courses taughts

Mathematics for Business Applications

   Textbook: Applied calculus, 5th Edition by Daniel D. Benice.

Mathematics for Business Application IU008A course teaches Undergraduate students to solve business problems independently. Topical coverage will include fundamental concepts of Antiderivatives and Indefinite Integrals, Differential equations and their applications, multi-variable functions and partial derivatives, and Constrained Optimization.

Organizational Behavior

   Textbook: Organizational Behavior by Stephen P. Robbins; Timothy A. Judge.

Understanding organizational behavior has never been more critical for managers. IUBM Organizational Behavior course provides students with a conceptual framework to understand the global environment where international businesses operate. This course also familiarizes undergraduate students with various political and cultural systems affecting global businesses' influence strategies.
